K-9 bot is a discord bot specially made
for the Bigger on the Inside server!

You can talk to this bot by putting K-9 before your messages,
or by trying one of our many slash commands!

Example:
User says: K-9 What does TARDIS stand for?
K-9 responds: Time And Relative Dimension In Space.
